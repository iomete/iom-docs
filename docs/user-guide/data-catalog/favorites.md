If you keep coming back to the same tables, bookmark them. The **Favorites** tab puts all your bookmarks in one place so you don't have to hunt through catalogs every time.

## Adding and Removing Favorites

Bookmarking a table takes one click. Open its [detail page](./table-details) and click the **Bookmark** button (star icon) in the header. The icon fills in to confirm the bookmark.

To remove a bookmark, click the star icon again on the detail page. You can also remove favorites from the **Favorites** tab using the delete icon next to each row.

<Img src="/img/data-catalog/bookmark-button.png" alt="Bookmark and Copy buttons in the table detail page header" />

## Viewing Your Favorites

Once you've bookmarked a few tables, the **Favorites** tab gives you quick access to all of them. Each row displays the **Table** name (linked to its detail page), along with its **Catalog** and **Schema**. The list paginates at 50 per page.

<Img src="/img/data-catalog/favorites-list.png" alt="Favorites tab showing a list of bookmarked tables with Table, Catalog, and Schema columns" />

Favorites are personal, scoped to your user and domain. Other users won't see yours.
