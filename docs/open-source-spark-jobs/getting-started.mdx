The Spark Jobs Marketplace gives you a library of pre-built jobs you can deploy in minutes instead of writing them from scratch. Browse **All apps** for built-in jobs covering ingestion, compaction, catalog sync, and more. Switch to **My apps** to publish your own job templates so others in your organization can discover and reuse them.

---

## Spark Jobs Marketplace

The Marketplace is where you'll find every built-in job IOMETE ships. Open the **Job Templates** page, then click **Marketplace** in the top-right corner.

## Available Jobs

Each card below links to a built-in job you can deploy today. Pick the one that matches your workload to see configuration details and examples.

For the latest version of each job, see the [Marketplace Jobs release notes](../deployment/on-prem/release-notes/marketplace-jobs.md).
